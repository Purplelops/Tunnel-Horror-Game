Tunnel horror game
- You have to make it down a tunnel while avoiding anomalies

Rules
- Turning around at any point will kill you

Story
- Anomalies exist because of a malfunction in a machine
- You are tasked with making it down the tunnel to reset the object
- The object is a stabilizer of the collective conciousness of humanity
- The thing that stabilizes the object is the concept of not knowing what it is
